About

I am Olufemi Olusina, a second year Bachelor of Information Technology student at Otago Polytechnic, Dunedin, New Zealand.

This portfolio outlines my individual report regarding the Software Engineering project - The Decision Spinner.

The Decision Spinner is a Web App designed by Mark Gowans, Grayson Orr and myself. It is made to assist the user with making decisions based on the question and options provided.
