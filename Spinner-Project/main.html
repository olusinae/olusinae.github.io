The Decision Spinner

About Us

Making decisions is something humans do without thought, without hesitation,
without worry every single minute of the day. In those moments when something
hangs in the balance, we are constantly hesitatating, self-doubting and
fearing our decisions we make.

Remember we are good at making decisions, after all us humans are making decisions
from the moment we wake up to the moment we go to bed.

Software Engineering students; Mark Gowans, Olufemi Olusina and Grayson Orr have
developed a small "Decision Making" web application which will help you make decisions
on everyday activities such as "What should I have for breakfast?" or
"How long will I nap for after Polytechnic?".
